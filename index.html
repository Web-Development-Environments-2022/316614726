My name is Clara, and I was born in Suceava, Romania in 1928 and I'm a holocaust survivor.
When I was 10 years old, on Sukkot holiday, the romanian military which was an accomplice of the Nazis, came to our house and took me and my family.
We took little food and cloths, just what we could carry, and aboarded on trains which took us to Mogilev on the Romania-Russia border, then we walked on foot all the way to Lucenec, Slovakia.
My father, who worked in trading, was able to earn some money, which he used to bring us back to Mogilev.
We stayed there until the Russian military conquered the city and free us from the Nazi regime.
In 1951, me and my family finally moved to Israel and lived in the "Maabarot" here.
Those were difficult times but we did manage.
I met my husband Abraham here and married him in 1952.
Today, I live in Rehovot, after I grew a family here on 2 children, 6 grandschildren and 8 great-grandchild.
I love to sew, cook and listen to Romanian and Idish music.

#1 : - Web1 was built as a "read-only" documents
- Web2 was built as a "read-write" docs
- Web3 was built as a "read-write-exectue" doc

#2 : - Web1 was built for companies for they can share their knowledge
- Web2 was built for people so they can communicate and build communities (social network)
- Web3 was built "for machines". Platform that provides enviroment for different softwares (SaaS, Metaverse...)

#3 : - Web1 search engines could only identify and look for Macro contents (words as written - by term freqency)
- Web2 search engines could also find micro content, like pics, audio, video, links and more...
- Web3 search engines could do more, and find more content by analyzing part of the ambiguity by homonyms, synonyms and general context

Q2

User Story: Searching for a disease
On web1 we would only find pages with general information about the disease. The most common pages would be such that the word of the disease appeared most in.
On web2 we could also find blogs and community pages of people that share their knowledge about it, videos and pictures...
More over, the result for general information would be more accurate and we can expand our query for better results for our use (like "disease-name side-affects")
On web3 we would also receive a SaaS app that provide us platform to make an online appointment with a special doctor.
